Sindhutai Sapkal was an Indian social worker who worked for the welfare of thousands of orphan children. She is bestowed with over 270 national and international awards which include the Nari Shakti Award presented to her by the President of India Ram Nath Kovind.

  • In 2012,"Real Heroes Awards", given by CNN-IBN and Reliance Foundation
  • In 2016,"Honorary doctorate", by the Dr. D. Y. Patil College of Engineering, Pune
